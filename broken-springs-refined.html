Do Not Attempt DIY Spring Repair

Garage door springs are under extreme tension. A snapped spring can cause serious injury or death. Always call a professional for spring-related issues.

Types of Garage Door Springs

Understanding which type of spring system your garage door uses helps us prepare the right replacement parts.

Torsion Springs

Located above your garage door opening, torsion springs wind and unwind to lift and lower your door. They're the most common type in Calgary homes.

  • Mounted horizontally above the door
  • Typical lifespan: 10,000+ cycles
  • Smoother, more balanced operation
  • Higher tension — more dangerous to repair

Extension Springs

Located on either side of the door, extension springs stretch to store energy. Often found in older Calgary homes and lighter doors.

  • Mounted on tracks above horizontal rails
  • Should have safety cables installed
  • Less expensive to replace
  • Can break suddenly without warning
