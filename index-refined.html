Do Not Attempt DIY Spring Repair

Garage doors are heavy — most residential doors weigh between 130 and 350 pounds. When springs or cables fail, that weight becomes unpredictable and dangerous.

Torsion springs store immense energy. If a spring snaps or is improperly handled, it can cause serious injury. This is why we never recommend DIY repairs.

Always call a professional for spring-related issues.
