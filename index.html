SAFETY WARNING: Do Not Attempt DIY Spring Repair

Garage doors are heavy — most residential doors weigh between 130 and 350 pounds. When springs, cables, or tracks fail, that weight becomes unpredictable and dangerous.

Torsion springs store immense energy. If a spring snaps or is improperly handled, it can cause serious injury or death. This is why we never recommend DIY repairs for spring or cable issues.

Security is compromised when your garage door won't close properly. An open or stuck door is an invitation for theft, especially if your garage connects to your home.

Don't take chances. Call a professional.
